Our History

    We are secondhand vinyl specialists with well over 20 years in the industry. With a love of music of all styles, our passion and focus is on the buying and selling of pre-loved vinyl records of most genres and vintage hi-fi equipment. Established in 2002, Licorice Pie has recently moved to new premises located at the Collingwood Arts Precinct.Our wide range of music and dedication to vinyl is something we’re famous for. From Afro to Zydeco we have something to suit almost everyone’s taste, and if you’re a collector we have extensive range of jazz, psych, prog, Australian and more.Our range of classical is second to none. We have fresh stock arriving from all corners of the globe and floor stock is updated regularly.
